Learn About Us

Dashme from the name is a concept of giving out things you have outgrown or are no longer fashionable to you. In every society there will always be two ends, a giver and a receiver and a giver who might be receiver at one point too. dashme has been created as a platform for givers and receivers to meet. AS an example we have people relocating and have some few good stuffs that might not neccesarily be sellable but will be of good use to somebody out there who needs them.

Any item of choice ranging from groceries,beauty products, clothes and furnitures will always be valuable to someone no matter how out of style they seem to you so far they are still in good condition. We all know how tough the country is, so being a helping hand with things you longer need won't be a bad idea and we are here to facilitate that. At Dashme, we value your gifts and we are greatful that you will be putting a smile on someone's face.


If you have any huge(out of the ordinary) item you want to give to any lucky person, kindly contact us and we will make it known unless you want to remain anonymous.
